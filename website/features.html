Platform Features

Discover the comprehensive suite of tools and capabilities that make our Oil & Gas Market Optimization platform powerful and versatile.

Custom Data Upload

Upload your own CSV or Excel files for each commodity. The system supports various data formats and automatically detects date columns and price data.

Automated Data Cleaning

The platform automatically handles missing values, outliers, and data inconsistencies to ensure high-quality analysis.

Sample Data Generation

Don't have your own data? Generate realistic sample data for all four commodities with just one click.

Feature Engineering

Automatically generate technical indicators and features for analysis, including moving averages, RSI, MACD, and Bollinger Bands.

Moving Average Crossover

Implement and backtest moving average crossover strategies with customizable fast and slow window parameters.

RSI Strategy

Use Relative Strength Index (RSI) for mean reversion trading with adjustable overbought and oversold thresholds.

Bollinger Bands Strategy

Implement Bollinger Bands strategies for volatility-based trading decisions.

Parameter Optimization

Fine-tune strategy parameters to maximize performance metrics like Sharpe ratio or total return.

Value at Risk (VaR)

Calculate VaR using multiple methodologies including Historical, Parametric, and Monte Carlo approaches.

Return Distribution Analysis

Visualize and analyze return distributions to understand market behavior and risk characteristics.

Monte Carlo Simulation

Run Monte Carlo simulations to project potential future price paths and assess strategy robustness.

Drawdown Analysis

Analyze maximum drawdowns and recovery periods to assess strategy risk.

Interactive Charts

Explore data and results through interactive charts with zoom, pan, and hover capabilities.

Performance Metrics Dashboard

View comprehensive performance metrics including total return, Sharpe ratio, volatility, and win rate.

Strategy Comparison

Compare multiple strategies side-by-side to identify the best approach for different market conditions.

Export Capabilities

Export data, charts, and analysis results in various formats including CSV, Excel, and PNG.
